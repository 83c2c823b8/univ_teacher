\(y= -x + 1\)のグラフを書いてみよう．
\begin{center}
  \begin{tabular}{c|c c c c c c c c c c c c} \hline
    \(x\) &\(\cdots\) & -4 & -3 & -2 & -1 & 0 & 1 & 2 & 3 & 4 & \(\cdots\) &\\ \hline
    \(y\) &           &    &    &    &   &   &   &   &            &\\ \hline
  \end{tabular}\\[10pt]

  \begin{tikzpicture}
    \draw[dotted,thin,step=0.5] (-3,-3) grid (3,3);
    \draw [->,thick] (-3.5,0) -- (3.5,0) node [right]{\(x\)};
